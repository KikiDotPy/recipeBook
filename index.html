Gingerbread Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1/4 cup butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Cream butter and molasses.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Roll out dough and cut into shapes.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes.

Hot Cocoa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan.
  2. Whisk in cocoa powder, sugar, and salt.
  3. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add vanilla and stir well.
  5. Serve with whipped cream or marshmallows.
