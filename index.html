Christmas Cookies from One Dough
Ingredients:
  • 250g butter (cold, cubed)
  • 250g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch of salt
Instructions:

1. Mix butter and sugar, then add eggs. Gradually incorporate flour and salt.
2. Chill dough for 30 minutes. Divide and flavor as desired.
3. Shape, bake at 180°C for 10–12 minutes, and decorate.

Fresh Pasta Dough
Ingredients:
  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
Instructions:

1. Make a flour mound, crack eggs into the center, and mix.
2. Knead for 10 minutes. Wrap and let rest for 30 minutes.

Carbonara
Ingredients:
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 3 egg yolks + 1 whole egg
  • 150g guanciale
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper to taste
Instructions:

1. Cook pasta, reserving water. Fry guanciale until crispy.
2. Toss pasta with guanciale. Add egg-cheese mixture and pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
