Struffoli

Certified by Kiki

Ingredients:

  • 250g butter (cold, cubed)
  • 250g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch of salt

Instructions:

Carbonara

Certified by Kiki

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti no. 3
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2-3 slices of guanciale (or high-quality bacon)
  • 1 additional egg yolk (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • 70g finely grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 20g finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • White wine, for deglazing

Instructions:

  1. Cut the guanciale into strips or cubes and brown slowly in a pan over low heat until crispy, being careful not to burn it.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Pecorino Romano, Parmesan, and black pepper to taste to create the egg mixture.
  3. Cook the pasta in boiling water until al dente. While the pasta cooks, remove the guanciale from the pan and set aside, leaving the rendered fat in the pan.
  4. Deglaze the pan with white wine, allowing the alcohol to evaporate completely.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the pan with the guanciale fat and wine. Toss over medium-low heat, adding a little pasta cooking water to help the pasta absorb the flavors.
  6. Remove the pan from heat (or keep on a very low flame) and add the egg mixture, stirring constantly to combine without overcooking the eggs.
  7. Once well combined, add the guanciale back to the pan, stir briefly, and serve immediately with an additional sprinkling of Pecorino or Parmesan cheese.
